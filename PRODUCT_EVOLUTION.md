# Aptema 0.4.0 Product Evolution

Before: a small brightness utility with controls.

After: a premium adaptive eye-comfort assistant.

Flow: install, enable Auto, use tray, correct rarely, let Aptema learn. Home answers whether comfort is active, what changed, why, and how to pause or correct it. Technical controls live under Displays, Applications, Profiles, Automation, Learning, and System.

0.4.0 adds the Adaptive Horizon identity, full light/dark/high-contrast shell, flyout, OSD with Undo, global Quick Adjust, guided setup, stable per-display control, custom profiles, process rules, deterministic automation, explainable decisions, confidence, local aggregate learning, startup recovery, private diagnostics, support bundles, transactional updates, and full Aptema technical naming.

Learning remains bounded by all safety limits. Low-confidence contexts cannot produce abrupt changes. Fullscreen games, video, and presentations stay protected. Legacy settings remain available for recovery and are never deleted automatically.
